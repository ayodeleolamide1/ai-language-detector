About Me

From Zero Code to Full-Stack AI

A story of self-directed learning, entrepreneurial drive, and building real technology for underserved languages.

👨🏾‍💻

Olamide Tosin Ayodele

// Founder @ Freedom Global Media

AI and social media enthusiast with a problem-solving orientation. Self-taught full-stack developer, data analytics learner, and digital marketing strategist. Based in Lagos, Nigeria — targeting Europe in 2025.

full-stack data-analytics digital-marketing NLP/AI Lagos → Europe

I'm the founder of Freedom Global Media, a registered technology and media company in Nigeria. In 2024, I set myself an impossible challenge: build a production-grade, AI-powered multilingual translation and transcription platform from absolute zero — no prior coding experience, no team, just curiosity and determination.

The result: a live, three-tier full-stack application serving 10 languages — including five African languages typically ignored by mainstream AI tools.

My frontend is deployed on Netlify. The backend (Node.js/Express) and AI engine (Python Flask + Azure Translator) are deploying to Railway. The platform includes real-time live call translation via WebSockets, voice I/O, and a custom phrase dictionary for Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Twi, and Xhosa.

I'm now building a matching data analytics and digital marketing portfolio to pursue a multi-track tech career across Europe — where my energy, self-directed learning, and cross-cultural perspective can make a real impact.

Affordable. Growing startup scene. Remote-friendly. Lower cost of entry.

Critical Skills Permit

Employer Work Permit

High salaries. Strong work-life balance. English widely spoken in tech.

Digital Nomad Visa

New digital nomad visa pathway. Growing remote-work ecosystem. Affordable cities.
